---
name: grilling
description: Stress-test a workshop test plan or product design through a one-question-at-a-time interview. Use when the user asks to grill, challenge, or clarify a direction before implementation.
---

Interview the user relentlessly until both sides share a precise understanding.

- Walk each branch of the design tree and resolve dependencies between decisions.
- Ask exactly one question at a time and wait for the answer.
- Include a recommended answer with every question.
- Look up facts in the repository instead of asking the user.
- Put product and design decisions to the user.
- Do not implement anything until the user confirms the direction.
